# Doing It — The Ultimate User Guide

Welcome to **Doing It**, your state-of-the-art Windows desktop productivity widget! Over time, we've packed this widget with incredibly powerful premium features. Because it lives as a floating, always-on-top overlay, you can access your entire life without ever switching windows.

This guide will walk you through **every single feature**, how to use it, and the secret keyboard shortcuts that make this app so fast.

---

## Navigation & The Window
**Doing It** operates as a beautiful, frosted-glass panel containing your Notes, Tasks, Reminders, Focus Timer, Calendar, and Insights.

### Screen Edge Docking:
- **Drag the Main Window** all the way to the left or right edge of your monitor. It will instantly "snap" and stretch to the full height of your screen (like a sidebar).
- Drag it away from the edge to undock it!

---

## Global Shortcuts (The "Power User" Tools)
You don't even need the window open to use these features. They work globally across Windows!

- **`Ctrl + Shift + C` (Clipboard Capture):** Copies whatever text you have highlighted in any app and saves it directly to your Notes. It even tags it with the name of the app you copied it from!
- **`Ctrl + Shift + N` (Toggle Visibility):** Instantly shows or hides the widget.

## 1. Notes (Tab 1)
Your brain dump. Notes act as your clipboard history and quick-capture center.

### Premium Markdown
Notes now fully support GitHub-style markdown. Double-click any note to edit it. When you click away, it instantly renders into beautiful formatting!
- Use `#` for large Headers.
- Use `**word**` for **bold** text.
- Use `*word*` for *italic* text.
- Use \`code\` to highlight inline blocks in premium purple.
- Use `> quote` for blockquotes.
- **Rich Links:** If you paste a URL, the app automatically reaches out to the internet, downloads the website's thumbnail and title, and replaces the boring text link with a gorgeous, clickable **Visual Card**.

### Image Pasting
Just `Ctrl+V` inside the Notes tab! Any image currently copied to your Windows clipboard will be saved locally and displayed as a beautiful thumbnail.

### Universal Hashtags
Type `#work` or `#ideas` anywhere inside a Note. The app automatically detects the tag, turns it into a purple clickable pill, and adds an interactive filter button to the top of your screen.

## 2. Tasks & Todos (Tab 2)
The command center for your day.

### The "My Day" Unified View
At the very top of your Tasks list, you'll see a special section called **"My Day"**. This automatically pulls together everything you have to do today:
- Tasks due today.
- Reminders scheduled for today.
- External Calendar events happening today.

### Smart Natural Language Scheduling
When creating a task, you don't need to manually click a calendar. Just type naturally!
- *Example:* "Finish the quarterly report tomorrow at 3pm"
- *Magic:* The app strips out the word "tomorrow", sets the exact due date in the background, and automatically schedules a system popup reminder for 3:00 PM tomorrow.

### Focus Integration
Click the play icon next to a task to instantly send it to the Focus Timer. The timer will display the task name, keeping you accountable!

### Drag-and-Drop
Click and hold a task. Drag it over the **Calendar Tab** icon, let the Calendar open, and drop the task onto a specific date block to instantly set its due date!

---

## 3. Focus Timer & Audio (Tab 3)
A native Pomodoro timer built to keep you in the zone.

### Features
- **Compact Analytics Card:** We've merged your daily session count, total focus time, and weekly progress chart into a single, beautiful dashboard directly below the timer.
- **Smart Pause:** If you lock your Windows computer (`Win + L`), the timer automatically pauses so you don't lose time when you step away!
- **Auto-DND:** The moment you click Start, the app tells Windows 11 to turn on **Focus Assist/Do Not Disturb**, suppressing all incoming notifications.
- **Mini-Timer:** Click the pop-out icon in the top right to detach the timer into a tiny, distraction-free pill you can put in the corner of your screen.

### Ambient Focus Audio
Look for the sleek audio pill below the playback controls. You can select **Deep Focus (Brown Noise)** or **Heavy Rain**. The moment you start the timer, high-quality ambient audio will fade in to boost your concentration. If you pause, it gracefully fades out.

## 4. Calendar (Tab 4)
Your eagle-eye view of the month.

### Syncing with Google/Outlook
1. Click the **Settings Gear** on the main window.
2. Paste the `.ics` secret address from your Google Calendar or Outlook into the External Calendar box.
3. Your calendar will now sync automatically every hour! You will see grey dots on the calendar grid representing your real-world meetings.
4. If a meeting has a Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet link, an automatic **"Join"** button will appear in the events list!

---

## 5. Insights Dashboard (Tab 5)
Your personal productivity analytics.

The 5th tab on the sidebar opens the **Insights Dashboard**. Here, the app does the math for you:
- **Weekly Velocity:** A beautiful CSS Bar Chart showing exactly how many tasks you completed on their due dates over the last 7 rolling days.
- **Stats:** Tracks your *Total All-Time Deep Focus Hours*, your total completed tasks, and automatically detects your most frequent daily mood based on the mood-prompts you answer when finishing tasks.

## The Command Palette (Ctrl + K)
This is the ultimate secret weapon for power users.
Press **`Ctrl + K`** while the main window is open to blur the background and summon the command line.

- **Search:** Start typing to instantly search through all your Notes and Tasks simultaneously.
- **Actions:** Type `>` to access app commands!
  - `> Start Timer`
  - `> Clear Completed Tasks`
  - `> Switch to Calendar`
  - `> Undock Window`

*Navigate using your Arrow Keys, and press Enter to execute!*
